# Hazemoor — Арт-біблія: промпти для генерації персонажів (Nano Banana)

> Створено 11.06.2026. Робочий процес: один персонаж = одна генерація.
> **ПРАВИЛО:** до КОЖНОГО промпта нижче в кінці додається незмінний STYLE BLOCK (внизу файла). Він — гарант єдиного арт-дирекшну серії.
> Еталони стилю вже згенеровані: Вартовий (ліхтар = key light) і Міа (очі = світло болота).

---

## STYLE BLOCK (копіювати в кінець кожного промпта)

```
Cinematography: shot on anamorphic 35mm, 40mm lens, full-body composition
with negative space of fog. Muted desaturated palette — iron grey, peat
black, bog green, bone white. Heavy atmosphere, volumetric fog, drizzle,
painterly realism in the style of dark fantasy concept art — textured
brushwork, no glossy video-game render, no anime, no heroic fantasy gloss,
no bright colors.
```

## Каен — шаман Тихого Шелесту

```
Cinematic full-body character portrait, dark fantasy. Kaen — an ELDERLY 
male of the Muri, an amphibious bog-folk race, NOT human. Shaman and healer 
of a stilt-village. He stands inside his medicine house: a dim hut of 
woven reeds and bone hooks, bundles of dried mint and swamp herbs hanging 
overhead, bark scrolls with carved script in the shadows behind him.

Physiology: old but unbowed, lean, upright. Skin near-black with a deep 
green sheen, dry and creased like old riverbed mud. Faded pale bog-paint 
shaman symbols on his forearms, repainted so many times over decades they 
have soaked into the skin. Subtle gill slits on his neck. Eyes yellow-green 
with vertical pupils, but DIMMED with age — the stare of someone who has 
kept one secret for twenty years. Long grey-streaked hair bound with bone 
rings.

Costume: layered healer's robes of fish-leather and reed fiber, a sash 
holding bone needles, clay vials, dried roots. One hand rests on a tall 
staff of black bog-oak topped with a knot of woven reeds. No metal on him.

Mood and light: a single shaft of grey daylight through the smoke hole as 
key light, warm faint ember glow from a clay hearth as fill. He is half 
turned toward the light, half in shadow — a man standing between truth and 
silence.
```

## Лілея (Алтея) — остання з Ключників

```
Cinematic full-body character portrait, dark fantasy. Lileya — a human 
sorceress in her late 30s, the last of the Keykeeper bloodline. She stands 
before the flooded gates of an ancient half-drowned monastery, knee-high 
black water perfectly still around her.

Face: severe, pale, ascetic; dark hair pulled back tightly; the controlled 
expression of someone whose family duty outlived her family. A fresh thin 
cut across her palm — her blood opens the ancient locks.

Costume: long dark travel robes, practical not ornate, hems wet and heavy 
with bog water; over them a faded ceremonial stole embroidered with 
angular rune patterns of her order, the threads tarnished. In one hand she 
holds her defining artifact: an ANCIENT BRONZE CYLINDER covered in carved 
runes, held like both a key and a relic. The runes on the cylinder emit a 
faint warm amber glow — the ONLY warm light in the frame.

Environment: massive sunken monastery gates behind her, stone covered in 
thousand-year moss, water line marks on the walls, fog hanging between 
broken columns.

Mood and light: cold grey-green ambient as key, the amber rune-glow of the 
cylinder as a small motivated accent under her hands and chin, thin cold 
rim light from above. She is not a warrior — she is a living key, and the 
frame should feel like a ritual, not an adventure.
```

## Ілія Марр — Вартова-Курат

```
Cinematic full-body character portrait, dark fantasy. Iliya Marr — a human 
woman around 30, a Wandering Warden-Curator: a lawkeeper-priestess hybrid. 
She stands on a rain-slick stone causeway outside an iron-walled city, 
upright, composed, duty incarnate.

Face: clear, disciplined, quietly grieving — the face of someone who chose 
law over her own blood. Short practical dark hair, rain on her skin, steady 
eyes.

Costume and artifacts: the same order as the male Warden but better kept — 
dark blackened half-plate over grey vestment-like robes, immaculately 
maintained yet clearly traveled. On her chest hangs a QUARTZ LANTERN OF THE 
CURATOR glowing with steady cold white light — the brightest light in 
frame, casting white underlight on her face. At her hip a consecrated 
longsword of pale white silver. Small crystal foci on her fingers and belt 
catching the lantern light. A warden's seal on a chain.

Environment: behind her the iron walls and stone spires of a fortress city 
in rain and fog; before her, the darkness of the moor — she stands exactly 
on the boundary line.

Mood and light: her quartz lantern as cold white key light, grey rainy 
ambient fill, faint warm windows of the city far behind as contrast. 
Composition mirrors the male Warden's portrait — they are two notes of the 
same order.
```

## Себастьян Марр / Блазень Фіпп — очільник Ордену Семи Кинджалів

```
Cinematic full-body character portrait, dark fantasy. The Jester Phipp — 
court fool of a royal palace who is secretly Sebastian Marr, grandmaster of 
a clandestine order. He stands in a dim marble throne hall, alone, caught 
in the exact moment of dropping the fool's act: posture straightening, the 
grin fading into something old and cold.

Face: a man in his 60s, sharp intelligent eyes that do not match the 
painted fool's face — faded ceremonial face-paint, half worn off, white 
and grey. The duality must read instantly: clown's surface, commander's 
bones.

Costume: a jester's motley — but in the Hazemoor palette: charcoal, bone 
white and dried-blood crimson diamonds, worn velvet, frayed gold thread. 
On his cap and cuffs hang small BELLS CAST OF WHITE SILVER, faintly 
luminous — sacred metal of the order he betrayed, hiding in plain sight. 
One hand behind his back; the other lazily holds a fool's scepter that 
casts the shadow of a DAGGER on the marble floor.

Environment: vast empty throne hall, white marble columns stained by age, 
a single high window of grey light, his long shadow stretching toward the 
empty throne. Hidden emblem detail: somewhere subtle — a ring or clasp — 
the sigil of two crossed daggers holding a circle with a drop in the 
center.

Mood and light: single cold window shaft as key, deep shadows, the white 
silver bells and the dagger-shadow as the storytelling accents. Court 
elegance gone stale; menace through stillness, not action.
```

## Серіт Келм — адміністративний слідчий

```
Cinematic three-quarter character portrait, dark fantasy. Serit Kelm — a 
human administrative investigator of indeterminate age between 40 and 60, 
the system given a face. He sits at a plain wooden table in a grey 
administration office, a steaming cup of tea and one closed report folder 
in front of him, hands folded, waiting politely.

Face: unremarkable on purpose — neat, clean-shaven, calm; the kind of face 
witnesses fail to describe. Eyes patient and absolutely unyielding, 
looking straight at the viewer as if the viewer is the one being 
interviewed.

Costume: a plain grey travel cloak without insignia over modest dark 
clothing; a leather-bound notebook at his elbow that has seen more fates 
than the city archive. No weapon anywhere — his weapon is the folder.

Environment: bureaucratic austerity — stone walls, one small window of 
grey light, document shelves in shadow, rain on the glass. Everything 
orderly to the point of menace.

Mood and light: flat soft grey window light as key — deliberately 
undramatic, interrogation-room honesty; the steam of the tea the only 
moving thing in the frame. The horror of the image is its calm.
```

## Руфін — той, хто повернувся порожнім

```
Cinematic full-body character portrait, dark fantasy. Rufin — a human 
cartographer in his late 40s, found at the edge of a forbidden glade after 
giving up something no one should carry. He SITS motionless on a fallen 
log at the waterline, hands open and empty on his knees, staring at the 
black water — not catatonic, simply emptied.

Face: gaunt, several days unshaven, ink stains on his fingers that years 
of work tattooed into the skin. The eyes are the core of the image: open, 
calm, focused on something a few seconds behind reality. A man present and 
absent at once.

Costume: practical traveler-scholar clothes ruined by the bog — a 
once-good coat stiff with dried peat mud, a map case on a strap lying 
unopened beside him, an open journal on the log with one unfinished line 
of writing.

Environment: the edge of a ritual glade — a perfect circle of dead 
grey-white grass behind him, unnaturally drained, with a single deep 
footprint pressed into the soil at its center. Still water before him 
reflecting nothing it should.

Mood and light: pale overcast key light, no warm tones at all, faint 
cold luminance from the dead grass circle. Composition: heavy negative 
space above him — the frame itself should feel emptied.
```

## Моур — свідомість болота (фінал еп. 1, Галявина)

```
Cinematic wide establishing shot, dark fantasy, cosmic-scale dread. MOUR — 
the consciousness of the great bog itself, partially surfacing at a 
forbidden glade at night. From the black mirror-still water rises an 
IMMENSE form: vaguely winged, wings folded close, its silhouette suggested 
more than shown — part colossal body, part landscape, surface like wet 
peat, root and ancient bone. NOT a monster, NOT a dragon: a fragment of an 
ancient consciousness that took a shape out of courtesy.

Composition: a tiny human figure with a cold white lantern stands at the 
waterline in the lower third of the frame — scale is the entire point. The 
form rises into the fog and does not end; the top of it dissolves into the 
night sky as if the sky itself were its back.

Details: faint green will-o'-wisps drift around the form like attendants; 
a ring of dead white grass on the shore; the water shows no ripples — its 
emergence breaks no physical rules because the rules are its own. Two dim 
yellow-green lights high in the silhouette, suggesting eyes that are 
barely interested.

Mood and light: near-monochrome night — peat black and deep bog green; the 
human's cold white lantern as the only honest light source; the wisps and 
the two distant eye-lights as accents. Volumetric fog, absolute stillness. 
The feeling: not threat, but the presence of a contract older than 
language.
```

## Лоен — посланець Ордену

```
Cinematic three-quarter character portrait, dark fantasy. Loen — a human 
negotiator in his 40s, the courteous face of a clandestine order. He 
stands in a wealthy Valkorn study by a tall window, offering the viewer a 
seat with one open hand — a gesture both polite and inescapable.

Face: impeccably groomed, pleasant, professionally unreadable; the 
faintest patient smile of a man whom ANY answer will satisfy. That is 
what makes him unsettling.

Costume: expensive understated dark clothing with not a single emblem or 
sign of allegiance — quality says power, absence of symbols says secrecy. 
A leather document folder under one arm. One ring with an unremarkable 
flat black stone.

Environment: a rich study — dark wood, ledgers, a decanter, a map of the 
moor region on the desk weighted by a small empty stone artifact replica. 
Grey daylight through tall windows, fog over the city roofs outside.

Mood and light: soft grey window key light, civilized and clean — the 
most domesticated frame of the whole series, and therefore the most 
quietly dangerous. Everything in order, nothing true.
```

## Дамар — ділок, що вкрав не те

```
Cinematic three-quarter character portrait, dark fantasy. Damar — a young 
human trade-guild hustler in his late 20s, in a dockside warehouse at 
night, caught mid-deal next to an opened crate. He grins at the viewer 
with the sincere charm of a man who genuinely does not understand why 
everyone is so serious.

Face: quick, clever, likable; a grin with too much confidence in it; 
restless eyes already counting profit.

Costume: a merchant apprentice's clothes upgraded with stolen taste — a 
good coat one size off, guild trinkets, too many rings of cheap silver. 
His hands hover over the crate's straw packing where a WRAPPED OBJECT 
faintly seeps cold grey-green light between the cloth folds — he has no 
idea what he is holding, and the light is already wrong.

Environment: dark dock warehouse — rope, crates, tar barrels, a shuttered 
lantern, masts and fog through the open loading door behind him.

Mood and light: warm dim lantern key on his face (the only warm light in 
the series — he still lives in the ordinary world), against the cold wrong 
glow from the crate below: two lights arguing over one man who notices 
neither.
```

## Тесса — Торговий квартал Валькорна

```
Cinematic three-quarter character portrait, dark fantasy. Tessa — a sharp 
human tradeswoman of Valkorn's market quarter, 30s, standing behind her 
stall-counter at dusk as the market closes. She leans on the counter with 
both hands, appraising the viewer exactly the way she appraises goods.

Face: handsome, weathered by commerce not weather; one raised eyebrow; 
the half-smile of someone who knows the real price of everything, 
including information.

Costume: practical layered merchant clothing — wool, leather money belt 
with many small pouches, fingerless gloves, a merchant's measuring chain 
worn like jewelry; everything functional, nothing accidental.

Environment: a market street after hours — closed stalls, hanging scales, 
wet cobblestone reflecting the last grey light, fog creeping in from the 
side streets between tall narrow stone houses.

Mood and light: cold dusk ambient with one warm hanging oil lamp above her 
counter as key — an island of commerce-light in a closing city. The frame 
should feel like the start of a negotiation.
```

## Одрін — палацовий архіваріус

```
Cinematic three-quarter character portrait, dark fantasy. Odrin — an 
elderly human palace archivist, 60s, deep inside a vast underground 
archive. He stands halfway up a ladder between towering shelves, one 
candle-lantern in hand, looking down at the viewer over his shoulder — 
startled, defensive, guilty about something he said in a tavern once.

Face: thin, papery skin, ink-and-dust stained fingers, spectacles pushed 
up on his forehead, the anxious eyes of a man who knows exactly which 
shelf is now empty.

Costume: faded archivist robes with worn elbows, a heavy ring of keys at 
his belt, document tubes under one arm, an old map peeking from his 
sleeve.

Environment: cathedral-scale archive in shadow — endless shelves, rolled 
maps, dust in the candlelight, one conspicuously EMPTY pedestal with a 
clean dust-shadow where something stood for centuries.

Mood and light: single warm candle-lantern as key against cold vast 
darkness, long shadows up the shelves. The empty pedestal must read — the 
crime scene is in the background of the portrait.
```

## Другорядні (короткі промпти)

### Брес — провідник по гетто
```
Cinematic three-quarter portrait, dark fantasy. Bres — a wiry human man in 
his 30s, a refugee from a bog-frontier town living three years in the new 
ghetto of Valkorn. He leans against a damp brick wall in a narrow alley, 
arms crossed, already watching the viewer before the viewer noticed him. 
Patched layered street clothes, sharp patient eyes, the stillness of a man 
who survives by seeing first. Environment: crowded ghetto alley — laundry 
lines, smoke, fog, distant palace spires framed ironically between 
rooftops. Light: grey ambient, one warm brazier glow from a side yard.
```

### Стетсон — королівський слідчий
```
Cinematic three-quarter portrait, dark fantasy. Stetson — a human royal 
investigator, 50s, caught where he did not expect to be met: a palace 
garden colonnade at dawn, coat over his arm, documents half-hidden against 
his chest. Disciplined bearing, tired honest eyes, jaw of a man preparing 
a legal war against people above his rank. Good but unostentatious 
clothing, a royal warrant seal on a chain tucked half out of sight. Light: 
pale dawn grey, long colonnade shadows; the composition slightly off-guard, 
as if the viewer surprised him mid-stride.
```

### Варрік — старший мисливець Мурі
```
Cinematic three-quarter portrait, dark fantasy. Varrik — an older male 
Muri hunter, NOT human: near-black skin with green sheen, subtle gill 
slits, yellow-green eyes with vertical pupils, grey-dusted hair. He stands 
at the edge of a stilt village pier at dusk, a long bone-tipped fishing 
spear grounded beside him, watching the tree line the way sentries watch 
borders. Scarred forearms with faded bog-paint marks, fish-leather hunter 
garb, a necklace of marsh-bird talons. Light: failing grey-green dusk, 
village hearth-glow behind him; his silhouette belongs to the watch, not 
the warmth.
```

### Сірра — молода мисливиця Мурі
```
Cinematic three-quarter portrait, dark fantasy. Sirra — a young female 
Muri hunter, NOT human: near-black skin with green sheen, subtle gill 
slits, bright yellow-green eyes with vertical pupils, quick curious 
expression — the one villager unafraid of strangers. She crouches on a 
mooring post above black water, balanced and casual, a short bone knife 
spinning loosely in her fingers, head tilted, mid-question. Simple reed 
and fish-leather garb, fresh bog-paint marks on her wrists. Light: soft 
grey day, water reflections dancing on her skin; the liveliest frame of 
the Muri set — youth inside an old world.
```

## Правила серії

- STYLE BLOCK додається в кінець кожного промпта без змін.
- Один персонаж = одна генерація. Колажі заборонені.
- Раса Мурі: завжди «NOT human» + капс на GILL SLITS / VERTICAL pupils.
- Єдине тепле світло в серії дозволене: свічка Одріна, лампа Тесси, ліхтар Дамара, жар вогнища Каена — теплота = побут, холод = болото й Орден.
- Невдалі деталі виправляти через edit існуючого зображення, не повною регенерацією.
